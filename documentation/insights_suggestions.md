## Key Business Insights

### 1. Status analysis 
- **Returned and Cancelled orders** together account for **41.41%** of total orders, which is a very high rate. This indicates a significant loss of potential value, so the reasons behind returns and cancellations should be investigated


### 2. Marketing Channels
- **Instagram** generated the **highest revenue** with 175,505.50 _(23.56%)_ and had the **lowest problem order rate** _(37.07%)_ among all channels. This channel should be treated as one of the main priorities.
- **Google** had the **highest AOV** at 1,107.75, but also showed the highest cancellation rate *(24.07%)*. The reasons behind cancellations should be investigated, but overall the channel looks strong.
- **Email** brought **a solid share of customers** (*20.2%*), but its **revenue** was slightly lower(*19.83% of total active revenue*) comparing to other channels. 
- **Facebook** and **Referral** showed weaker performance compared to the other channels. They should be analyzed more deeply and potentially reworked.

### 3. Coupon Impact
- **SAVE10** had the best **Active Revenue** and **AOV**. It should be kept as the main promo code.
- **FREESHIP** performed well in terms of customer aquisition. However its **AOV** is very close to **No Coupon**. It is worth checking whether it remains profitable after including shipping costs.
- **WINTER15** should be reworked or replaced due to its ineffectivness. It had the weakest perfornace across the main coupon metrics.

### 4. Product analysis
- **Printer** and **Chair** were the strongest products by **Active Revenue**. They account for **around 32.4% of total active revenue**. **Chair** also had the highest **AOV** across products. These products should be highlighted as main revenue drivers. Bundle stratagies with weaker products could also be a good business decision. 
- **Printer** profitability should be checked because it had the highest *Coupon Usage %*.

### 5. Product Problem Analysis
- For **Monitor, Tablet and Laptop**, the reasons for returns should be investigated, since they had the highest *Return Rates*.
- For **Chair and Monitor**, the reasons for cancellations should be investigated.
